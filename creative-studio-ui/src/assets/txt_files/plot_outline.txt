EXAMPLE OUTLINE FOR "THE LOST MOUNTAIN"
=======================================

ACT 1: SETUP
- Thorin learns of ancestral treasure
- Driven by honor and family legacy
- Mountain setting with dragon threat
- Initial quest begins

ACT 2: CONFRONTATION
- Journey with companions
- Battles and challenges along the way
- Internal conflicts about greed vs honor
- Betrayal or loss of ally

ACT 3: RESOLUTION
- Final battle with dragon
- Treasure reclaimed but at great cost
- Thorin questions the price of victory
